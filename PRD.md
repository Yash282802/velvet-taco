# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Velvet Taco Website
## Introduction
The Velvet Taco website aims to provide an online presence for the taco business, allowing customers to learn about the brand, view menus, and place orders online. This document outlines the business goals, target audience, required pages, features, and success metrics for the website.

## Business Goals
The primary business goals for the Velvet Taco website are:
* Increase brand awareness and establish an online presence
* Drive sales through online ordering and promotions
* Provide a seamless customer experience, encouraging repeat business and positive word-of-mouth
* Gather customer feedback and insights to inform business decisions

## Target Audience
The target audience for the Velvet Taco website includes:
* Demographics: Foodies, young adults, and families with a medium to high disposable income
* Interests: Mexican cuisine, tacos, and casual dining
* Behaviors: Active online users, frequent diners, and individuals seeking convenient food options
* Psychographics: People valuing unique flavors, high-quality ingredients, and a fun dining experience

## Pages Needed
The following pages are required for the Velvet Taco website:
* **Home**: Introduction to the brand, featuring promotions, and calls-to-action (CTAs) for online ordering and menu viewing
* **Menu**: Detailed menu with descriptions, prices, and images of dishes
* **Locations**: List of restaurant locations, including addresses, phone numbers, and hours of operation
* **About**: Company history, mission statement, and team profiles
* **Contact**: Contact form, email address, and phone number for customer inquiries and feedback
* **Order Online**: Integrated online ordering system, allowing customers to place orders for pickup or delivery
* **Promotions**: Page showcasing current promotions, discounts, and limited-time offers

## Features
The Velvet Taco website will include the following features:
* **Responsive Design**: A mobile-friendly design, ensuring a seamless user experience across devices
* **Online Ordering System**: Integration with a third-party ordering platform or development of a custom solution
* **Menu Filtering and Sorting**: Ability for customers to filter and sort menu items by category, price, and dietary restrictions
* **Promotions and Discounts**: Automated promotion and discount system, with options for customers to receive exclusive offers via email or social media
* **Customer Reviews and Ratings**: Integration with review platforms, allowing customers to leave feedback and rate their experiences
* **Social Media Integration**: Links to Velvet Taco's social media profiles, enabling customers to share their experiences and interact with the brand
* **Newsletter Signup**: Option for customers to subscribe to a newsletter, receiving updates on promotions, new menu items, and events

## Success Metrics
The success of the Velvet Taco website will be measured by the following key performance indicators (KPIs):
* **Website Traffic**: Number of unique visitors, page views, and bounce rate
* **Online Ordering Conversion Rate**: Percentage of website visitors who complete an online order
* **Customer Engagement**: Social media engagement, email open rates, and customer review submissions
* **Sales Growth**: Increase in sales revenue, attributed to online ordering and promotions
* **Customer Retention**: Percentage of repeat customers, measured by online ordering and loyalty program participation

## Technical Requirements
The Velvet Taco website will be built using:
* **Content Management System (CMS)**: WordPress or a similar platform, allowing for easy content updates and management
* **Front-end Framework**: React or a similar framework, ensuring a responsive and interactive user interface
* **Back-end Framework**: Node.js or a similar framework, handling server-side logic and integration with third-party services
* **Database**: MySQL or a similar database management system, storing customer data, orders, and menu information
* **Payment Gateway**: Integration with a secure payment gateway, such as Stripe or PayPal, for online ordering and payment processing

## Timeline and Milestones
The development of the Velvet Taco website is expected to take approximately 12 weeks, with the following milestones:
* **Week 1-2**: Project planning, requirements gathering, and technical specifications
* **Week 3-6**: Design and front-end development
* **Week 7-10**: Back-end development, integration with third-party services, and testing
* **Week 11-12**: Launch preparation, deployment, and post-launch review

## Budget and Resources
The budget for the Velvet Taco website development is estimated to be $50,000, allocated as follows:
* **Design and Front-end Development**: $15,000
* **Back-end Development and Integration**: $20,000
* **Testing and Quality Assurance**: $5,000
* **Project Management and Coordination**: $10,000

The development team will consist of:
* **Project Manager**: Responsible for coordinating the project, ensuring timely completion, and managing the budget
* **Designer**: Responsible for creating the visual design and user interface
* **Front-end Developer**: Responsible for developing the client-side logic and user interface
* **Back-end Developer**: Responsible for developing the server-side logic, integrating with third-party services, and ensuring data security
* **Quality Assurance (QA) Engineer**: Responsible for testing the website, identifying bugs, and ensuring a smooth user experience
